Flare

Overview

Flare is an e-commerce platform that specializes in high-quality fashion items with a focus on trendy, sustainable products. My involvement in the project included designing the brand identity, website UI/UX, product packaging, and social media assets. The aim was to create a visually striking, user-friendly experience that would increase conversion rates and enhance Flare’s presence in the competitive online fashion market.


Objective

Develop a modern, sophisticated brand identity for Flare that resonates with fashion-conscious and environmentally aware consumers. Design a seamless, intuitive website that enhances the shopping experience and boosts conversions. Create engaging social media graphics and product packaging to build brand recognition and support sales.


My Role

Crafting the layout, user flow, and design elements of the e-commerce website to make shopping easy and enjoyable for customers. Designing eye-catching social media graphics to promote new products, sales, and campaigns, with a focus on Instagram and Facebook.


Visuals

Results

Flare’s Instagram following grew by 50%, with posts using my designs receiving 20% higher engagement compared to previous campaigns. Positive feedback from customers who praised the site’s design and user experience, leading to higher repeat purchases.
