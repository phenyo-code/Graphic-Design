Alchemy

Overview

Alchemy is a creative project I worked on to develop branding and promotional materials for a modern, up-and-coming lifestyle brand. The project involved designing logos, packaging concepts, social media graphics, and event materials to establish a unique and captivating identity that resonated with Alchemy’s audience of trendsetters and innovators.


Objective

Craft a brand identity that reflects Alchemy’s ethos of transformation, innovation, and style. Create marketing materials that drive audience engagement and brand recognition. Ensure a consistent visual language across all touchpoints, from digital to print.


My Role

Conceptualizing a versatile and memorable logo to serve as the cornerstone of Alchemy’s brand. Developing sleek and modern packaging concepts for the brand's products. Designing visually engaging content for Instagram and Facebook to build online presence and drive engagement. Creating banners, flyers, and promotional items for Alchemy’s product launch event. Collaborating closely with the Alchemy team to align designs with their vision and values.


Visuals

Results

Increased social media followers by 40% within the first month of launching the new visuals. Social media posts with my graphics saw an average engagement rate of 12%, surpassing the industry average. The event materials contributed to a sold-out product launch, with over 300 attendees and glowing feedback about the brand presentation. The Alchemy team praised the cohesive branding and reported improved recognition within their target market.
